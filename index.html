web design / development

    by Chris Rune

Thanks for visiting my portfolio.

I am arriving to the scene somewhat late in life, having spent my twenties studying and writing poetry, getting (and then being) married, traveling and baking bread. Over those years, I have worked as a teacher, tutor, legal writer and a string of other odd jobs, but nothing ever felt quite right for me. While I have always had an affinity for computers (and math to some extent), it was only recently that I stumbled into web development. Learning through Free Code Camp over the past several months, I (hope) to have found the right place for myself. As a developer, I can harmonize my creative and analytic capacities. In many ways, I am still working with language (whether English or Javascript, lines of verse or lines of code) and how to use the available tools to express something unique.

Below, you will find examples of my completed projects. I am afraid that -as yet- there is nothing especially original, but I hope to find my voice in time. Toward this end, I have occassionally strayed from current design trends, often to my own detriment. However, if my stubbornness has earned me nothing but ugly failures, at least they are my ugly failures, and you will forgive them.
